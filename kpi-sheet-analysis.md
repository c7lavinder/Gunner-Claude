# KPI Sheet Analysis

## Sheet Tabs Identified
1. **Scoreboard** - Main overview/summary
2. **AM Spotlight** - Acquisition Manager metrics
3. **LM Spotlight** - Lead Manager metrics  
4. **LG Spotlight** - Lead Gen metrics
5. **CC Campaign INPUT** - Cold Calls campaign data
6. **SMS Campaign INPUT** - SMS campaign data
7. **Deals** - Deal tracking
8. **Forms Campaign INPUT** - Web forms campaign data
9. **PPL Campaign INPUT** - Pay Per Lead campaign data
10. **JV Campaign INPUT** - Joint Venture campaign data
11. **PPC Campaign INPUT** - Pay Per Click campaign data
12. **Cards Campaign INPUT** - Postcards campaign data
13. **Referrals Campaign INPUT** - Referrals campaign data

## Scoreboard Structure (Main Tab)
- **P&L Section** - Lists channels: Cold Calls, SMS, Postcards, Webforms, PPL, PPC, JV's, Referrals
- **KPIs Section** - Tracks per channel:
  - $ Spent
  - to Lead (cost per lead)
  - to Offer (cost per offer)
  - to Contract (cost per contract)
  - to Deal (cost per deal)
  - $ Spent total
  - # Sent (volume)
  - Leads
