# 📊 GoHighLevel (GHL) CRM Guide — All Roles

> GHL is our **single source of truth.** Every lead, call, note, and stage change lives here. If it's not in GHL, it didn't happen.

---

## 📈 Pipeline Stages Explained

| Stage | What It Means | Who Owns It | When to Move Here |
|---|---|---|---|
| **New Lead** | Just entered the system | LG / System | Auto on contact creation |
| **Warm** | Some interest or motivation detected | Lead Manager | After conversation with openness but not ready for apt |
| **Hot** | Strong motivation, wants to hear an offer | Lead Manager | Motivation confirmed, price somewhat aligned |
| **Pending Apt** | Walkthrough scheduled | LM (sets) / Kyle (attends) | Specific date + time locked |
| **Walkthrough** | Kyle has visited the property | Kyle | After walkthrough completed |
| **Offer** | Offer presented to seller | Kyle | When offer is formally communicated |
| **Under Contract** | Seller accepted, agreement signed | Kyle / Corey | Signed agreement in hand |
| **Purchased** | Deal closed, we own it | Operations | After closing complete |

### Follow-Up Stages

| Stage | When | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **1 Month Follow Up** | Not ready now, had some interest | One contact attempt, then → 4 Month |
| **4 Month Follow Up** | Still not ready at 1 month | One contact attempt, then → 1 Year |
| **1 Year Follow Up** | Long-term nurture | One contact attempt, then → Archive |

## 📝 How to Add Notes

**Every interaction gets a note.** Every call, text, voicemail, email — log it.

### ✅ What to Include

- Date and time of interaction
- Who you spoke with (seller, spouse, family member)
- What was discussed — motivation, condition, price expectations, timeline
- What the next step is
- Specific details (repairs mentioned, emotional state, competing offers)

### ✅ Good Note Example

> **2/12/26 — Called seller.** Spoke with James. Property at 512 Maple has been vacant 8 months. Roof is shot, HVAC not working. Inherited from his father. Wants to sell but concerned about getting lowballed. Mentioned hoping for $150K. Explained our process. Open to walkthrough. Set apt for 2/15 at 10 AM with Kyle. Mood: cautious but engaged.

### ❌ Bad Note Example

> "Called. Left VM."

That tells us nothing. What number? What did the VM say? When are you following up?

## 🏷️ Tagging

Tags help us segment and filter leads. **Use them consistently.**

| Tag | When to Use |
|---|---|
| `Hot Lead` | High motivation, ready to move |
| `DNC` | Requested removal from call list |
| `Callback [Date]` | Requested callback on specific date |
| `Inherited` | Inherited property |
| `Vacant` | Property is vacant |
| `Landlord` | Property is a rental |
| `Foreclosure` | Facing foreclosure |
| `PPL - [Source]` | Lead from a pay-per-lead platform (e.g., `PPL - Leadzolo`) |

## 🚨 What NOT to Do in GHL

### ❌ Don't skip pipeline stages

A lead should **never** jump from New Lead to Under Contract. Each stage is a real step.

### ❌ Don't move to follow-up without working the cadence

"No answer" on first attempt ≠ 1 Month Follow Up. Work the full follow-up cadence first.

### ❌ Don't leave blank notes

Every stage move needs a note explaining why. Every call needs a note about what happened.

### ❌ Don't create duplicate contacts

**Search for phone number and address first.** Duplicates = confusion + missed follow-ups.

### ❌ Don't forget to set tasks

Next step exists? **Create a task with a due date.** Don't rely on memory.

### ❌ Don't delete contacts

Even dead leads stay in the system. Mark them appropriately, but never delete.

## 📅 Daily CRM Habits

| When | What |
|---|---|
| **Start of day** | Check tasks and pipeline for today's follow-ups |
| **After every call** | Log the note **immediately** — not "later," not "end of day" |
| **As things change** | Update pipeline stages in real time — don't batch |
| **End of day** | Review your pipeline — anything stuck? Follow-ups missed? |
| **Weekly** | Clean up notes, make sure everything is current |
