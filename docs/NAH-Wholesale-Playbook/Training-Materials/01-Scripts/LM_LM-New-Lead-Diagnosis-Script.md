**Role:** Lead Manager

1. Intro
Intro: Hello (Seller) **Pause** it’s NAME with New Again Houses **City**, I’m calling you because you spoke/texted with my assistant about your property at (123 Main St)
**Pause** “Probably caught you at a bad time.”
If “No”--> Go to set expectations
2. Setting Expectations
Thanks for taking my call,  is it okay if I share with you how we work and how we can get you an offer before we get started? I am just going to ask you some questions about your property and situation, if you feel we are not a fit at any time, please let me know and I will do the same. seem fair?
If we are a good fit then I will get a call scheduled with our franchise owner who will present you with the number we could purchase your property for. Is that okay?

3. Confirm Property Condition 
-Confirm Beds and bath i.e. “This is a 3/2 right?”
-Have you done any updates or projects that you think I should know about? 
-age of roof and HVAC (need to figure out if out if older than 10 years).  
-anything we would be scared about if we walked through the property?
4. Occupancy- 
-Are you currently living in the property, tenant occupied, or vacant? 
-Have the tenants complained about anything? Are there issues with the house you are dealing with while living there?
If tenant occupied, ask if there is a lease or month to month? 
**If there is a lease, ask when does it expire, and if it possible to get tenants out before closing**
5. Timeline- 

If we agreed on price today, How soon were you looking to start the process of selling your home?
Ex. less than 30 days, 2–3 months, or 60 plus 
If we had the price ready for you tomorrow, would you be able to give me a yes or no?
