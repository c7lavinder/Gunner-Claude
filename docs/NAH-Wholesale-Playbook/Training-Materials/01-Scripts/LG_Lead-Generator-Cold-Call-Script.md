# Lead Generator Cold Call Script

**Role:** Lead Generator
**Category:** Script

> Cold call script for Lead Generators. 

---

Cold Calling Script
1. Introduction
Caller: "Hey {Seller Name}, (pause) It’s (your name). I’ve been trying to reach the owner
of {property address}. You’re probably not the owner?"
2. Initial Qualification
If they are the owner:
Caller: "Okay, great. I’m calling you because we are looking to buy a property in your
area. You probably wouldn’t be interested in selling your property on {street name}?"
• If they are interested in selling: Transition to "Setting Expectations."
• If they are not interested in selling:
Caller: "Would there be a time in the future where you’d even consider selling your
property?"
• If they still say no:
Caller: "I totally understand that. Just so you know, we give a $2,000 referral to
anyone who sends us a property that we end up buying. I assume you're probably
not interested in that?"
3. Setting Expectations
Caller: "That’s great. I’m working with a private investor who’s looking to buy
properties in {City}. They do buy them for all cash, as-is, and at no closing cost. I just need
to ask a few questions about your property and situation to find out if we are a good fit to
work together or not. Sound fair?"
4. Property and Seller Information
Property Condition
• Confirm beds and baths.
• "Have there been any recent updates done or big issues that I should know about?"
• "Are you currently living in the property, is it tenant-occupied, or vacant?"

-- 1 of 5 --

• "Are there any repairs needed on the property?"
• If tenant-occupied: "Is there a lease or is it month-to-month?"
• If there is a lease: "When does it expire, and could you give the tenants a 60-day
notice?" (Confirm local laws, e.g., GA law).
Timeline
• "If we agreed on the price today, how soon were you looking to start the process of
selling your home?" (e.g., less than 30 days, 2-3 months, or 60+ days)
Influencers
• "Is there anyone who has input on the decision to sell the property?"
• "Okay, but does anybody have veto power to stop you from selling?"
Motivation
• "(Seller name), I know we have been on the phone for a couple of minutes now, what
even had you looking to sell the property?"
• If no motivation: "Okay, so other than us calling, you probably do not have a reason to
sell?"
• If NO, go to Price.
Diving Deeper into Motivation
• "It seems like..."
• "It feels like..."
• "It sounds like..."
• "How long has this been going on?"
• "Can you tell me more about that?"
• "What have you tried to do about it?"
• "When you say (repeat any sign of pain), what do you mean?"
• "How long have you been thinking about this?"
• "How’s that make you feel?"
• "If you do decide to sell, where do you think you might go? Why there?"
5. Pricing Discussion

-- 2 of 5 --

Initial Price Inquiry
1. Caller: "Okay, how much were you looking to get for the property?"
2. If they don't give a price: "Gotcha... So you probably don't have a range you would
want to get?"
3. If they still don't give a price: "Gotcha, in order to have the best chance of working
together, I'm assuming not even like a 10-20k price range? That way we can assist you
more effectively."
Price Anchoring
Caller: "So I just want to let you know, I see other investors—and keep in mind this is by no
means our offer—but other investors are buying properties at 40% to 50% of the
Zillow/Comparable Value. Again, that is not our offer, just what other investors are buying
properties for in cash. What would you say if they offered you something like that?"
• If they say it's "WAY TOO LOW" or "hell no":
Caller: "Again, like I said, that is not our offer; that is just what other investors are
buying properties at. What were you hoping I was going to at least say?"
• If they say "Yes, that would work":
Caller: "Okay {Seller Name}, I do think we could be a great fit to work together. With
that being said, if you have an extra 5-10 minutes, I would love to get you transferred
over to my home buying specialist, who could formally present you with that offer. Is
that okay?"
If Price is Given
Caller: "Thank you for that. Is that the best you can do? (PAUSE) I want to make sure so
when I present the number to my finance department, it will have the best chance of being
accepted."
• If they say "yes": Go to Next Call Expectations.
• If they say "no":
Caller: "Gotcha, what were you thinking?"
• If they drop their price and have slight to heavy motivation, go to "If We Are a Fit."
6. Next Steps
If Not a Fit

-- 3 of 5 --

Caller: "Okay, I believe I have all the information I need. Right now, {Seller First Name}, I do
not think we are the best fit. I think your best option to get the highest price would just be
to list it with a realtor or hold onto the property. We will follow up with you in the next 30-60
days just to check in. If something ever changes, you have my phone number and you can
call or text me anytime. Save my number so you recognize it in the future. Thank you."
If We Are a Fit (Meaning, Give an Offer)
Caller: "Okay, I believe I have all the information I need right now. With that being said, I do
believe we would be a great fit to work together. I would love to get you an offer for your
property. I just need to send this to finance, which takes about 30 minutes. Are you
available then for a conversation?"
• If not available in 30 mins: "Okay, are you available at {time} or {time}?"
Caller: "All I ask from you is that you make a confident yes or no decision, and NO is
perfectly okay. Also, if there are any other individuals that have input on the sale of the
property, they should be on the call too. If everything looks good and you like our offer,
would you be in the position to go over an agreement with me then?"
• If they ask for more information or a website:
Caller: "If you would like, I can share our website with you, where you can see some
testimonial reviews on us. Is that okay? website.com"
• If not available at the proposed time:
Caller: "Great, will you be available at {time} or {time} for {Acquisition Manager Name}
to give you a call to present you with that offer?"
• If they give a time: "Perfect, and during the call, if you liked our offer, what would
happen next?"
• If yes, then proceed to close out the call.
• If not: "I understand. When is a better time that will work for you to be able to
do that?" Then schedule the appointment at the new time provided.
7. Objection Handling
• "I have a tenant in place..."
• "Okay, so what are the tenant's lease terms—month-to-month or a set lease?" and
"You must be telling me that for a reason."
• "I don't need to sell."
• "Should we even continue talking? We are not always a good fit."

-- 4 of 5 --

• "I can list it on the market (MLS) and it will sell today."
• "Why have you not listed it on the market?"
• SMS Lead: asking price over or at market value
• Go into motivation ASAP.
• "How’d you get my info?"
• "I do not know how we got your number. My manager just gives me a list of numbers
to call. Do you want to sell your property?"
• "Why do you keep calling me?"
• "We want to see if you are looking to sell your house. You probably do not want to."
• "I’m currently fixing it up myself."
• "Sounds like everything has been going well with the remodel. You probably do not
want to sell to someone like us then?"
