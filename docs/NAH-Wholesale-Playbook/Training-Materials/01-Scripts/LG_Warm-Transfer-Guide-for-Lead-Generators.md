# 🤝 Warm Transfer Guide for Lead Generators

> A warm transfer is when you connect a live, interested seller directly to a Lead Manager while you're still on the line. You introduce them, pass key info, and drop off. The seller never feels "dumped" — they feel **handed off to someone who can help.**

---

## ✅ When to Transfer

Transfer when the seller:

- Confirms they'd **consider selling**
- Mentions any **motivation** (behind on payments, inherited, divorce, tired landlord, relocating, vacant, can't afford repairs)
- Asks about the **process** or what we can offer
- Says **"yeah, I'd be open to hearing a number"**
- Is **engaged and talking** — even if cautious

---

## ❌ When to Keep Dialing

Keep dialing when the seller:

- Says "not interested" and **won't engage** after your redirect
- Asks to be **removed from the list** (DNC — log and move on)
- Is clearly **not the property owner**
- Has **no motivation and no interest** — happy in the home, no reason to sell
- Is **hostile or abusive**

---

## ⚠️ Gray Area — Set a Callback Instead

| Situation | Action |
|---|---|
| "Call me back next week" | Schedule it in GHL, don't transfer |
| "Might be interested but not right now" | Schedule follow-up, add notes |
| "Let me talk to my spouse first" | Schedule callback for 2-3 days out |
| Seller is interested but LM isn't available | Take detailed notes, set a task for the LM |

---

## 📋 What Info to Pass to the Lead Manager

Before you transfer, collect and relay these details. Think of it as a **scouting report.**

| Info | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| **Seller's name** | So the LM can use it immediately |
| **Property address** | Confirms which property we're discussing |
| **Motivation** | The #1 thing — why might they sell? |
| **Timeline** | Looking to move soon? No rush? |
| **Property condition** | Any mentions of repairs, damage, vacancy |
| **Seller's mood/tone** | Warm, guarded, emotional? Helps LM prepare. |
| **Anything they asked about** | Price? Timeline? Process? |

---

## 🎯 The Transfer — Step by Step

### Step 1: Brief the LM (15-20 seconds, seller on hold)

> **You:** "Hey Daniel, I've got Mrs. Johnson on the line. She's got a property at 412 Elm Street. She inherited it from her dad who passed last year. It's been sitting vacant — she mentioned it needs a new roof and the HVAC is out. She seems open but a little cautious. She asked how fast we could close."

### Step 2: Introduce the Seller

> **You:** "Mrs. Johnson, I really appreciate you taking the time to talk with me. I've got Daniel on my team — he works directly with homeowners in your situation and can go into more detail about how our process works and what we could offer. Let me bring him in real quick."

### Step 3: Three-Way Intro

> **You:** "Daniel, this is Mrs. Johnson. She's got the property on Elm Street we were just talking about. Mrs. Johnson, Daniel's going to take great care of you."

### Step 4: Drop Off

You're done. Let the LM take it from here.

---

## 🚫 When the LM Isn't Available

Don't lose the lead. Here's what to do:

1. **Tell the seller:**
   > "My colleague is with another homeowner right now, but I want to make sure he calls you back today. What's the best number and time?"

2. **In GHL:** Create the contact (if new). Add detailed notes. Move to appropriate stage. Set a task for the LM.

3. **Message the LM directly:** Give them a heads-up so it doesn't get buried.

---

## 🚨 Common Transfer Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| **Transferring too early** — seller barely said hello | Make sure there's actual motivation or openness first |
| **Transferring with no context** — LM starts from scratch | Always brief the LM before connecting |
| **Cold transferring** — no introduction, seller confused | Always do a three-way intro |
| **Overselling before transfer** — "He'll give you an AMAZING offer!" | Set expectations, not hype: "He'll walk you through how it works" |
| **Not logging anything** — no notes in GHL | Always log the call and your notes, even if the transfer goes through |

---

## 📋 Quick Reference

1. **Transfer when there's real motivation or openness** — not just because they answered the phone
2. **Brief the LM in 15-20 seconds** — name, address, motivation, condition, mood
3. **Always do a three-way intro** — never cold-transfer a seller
4. **If the LM isn't available, don't lose the lead** — take info, log notes, set a task
5. **Log everything in GHL** — every transfer attempt, every note, every detail
