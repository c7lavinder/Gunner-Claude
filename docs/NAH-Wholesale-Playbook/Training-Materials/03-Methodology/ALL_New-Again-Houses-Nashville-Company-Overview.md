# 🏢 New Again Houses Nashville — Company Overview

> We buy houses directly from homeowners — **as-is, for cash.** We then either renovate and sell them, or sell them to other investors. This is **wholesale real estate.**

---

## 💡 How Wholesale Real Estate Works

1. **We find homeowners** who want or need to sell — often in situations where listing isn't ideal (needs repairs, need to move fast, inherited property, financial hardship)
2. **We make a cash offer** — as-is, no repairs, no showings, no agent fees
3. **We close fast** — often in 2-3 weeks
4. **After we buy it,** we either renovate and sell on the market, or wholesale to another investor

**Key distinction:** We're not agents. We're not brokers. **We're the buyer.** When we make an offer, it's our money on the table.

## ✅ What We Offer Sellers

| Benefit | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Buy as-is** | No fixing, no cleaning, no repairs. Leave the junk. |

## 👥 Who We Help

Our typical sellers are in situations like:

- 🏚️ **Inherited property** — got a house from a family member, don't want to deal with it
- 🔧 **Major repairs needed** — roof, foundation, HVAC gone, can't afford to fix
- 💸 **Financial hardship** — behind on mortgage, facing foreclosure
- 💔 **Divorce** — need to split assets and move on
- 😩 **Tired landlords** — done with tenants and maintenance
- ✈️ **Relocating** — moving for work, need to sell fast
- 🏗️ **Vacant property** — paying taxes/insurance on an empty house
- 👴 **Downsizing** — house is too much to maintain

## 🎯 Our Values

### Respect the Seller's Situation

Many sellers are going through difficult times. We treat everyone with empathy and respect. We never pressure, manipulate, or take advantage. We offer a fair solution and let them decide.

### Be Transparent

We tell sellers exactly how we arrive at our numbers. We explain the process. No hidden fees, no surprises at closing. If a deal doesn't work for us, we say so honestly.

### Be Professional

Show up on time. Follow through. Communicate clearly and promptly. Represent the company well on every call and at every property.

### Speed Matters

Time is often the #1 factor for our sellers. We move fast on everything — returning calls, making offers, scheduling walkthroughs, closing deals.

## 📊 The Pipeline (How a Deal Moves)

| Stage | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **New Lead** | Just entered the system. Not contacted yet. |
| **Warm** | Had a conversation, some interest detected. |
| **Hot** | Strong motivation, interested in hearing an offer. |
| **Pending Apt** | Walkthrough appointment is scheduled. |
| **Walkthrough** | Kyle has visited the property. |
| **Offer** | Offer has been presented. |
| **Under Contract** | Seller accepted. Paperwork signed. |
| **Purchased** | Deal closed. We own it. |

**Follow-up stages:** 1 Month → 4 Month → 1 Year — for leads that aren't ready now but might be later.
