| **Wholesale Fee** | ~$10K minimum |

### 📋 Seller Profile
- Motivated seller (hardship, inherited, tired landlord, divorce, relocation, etc.)

- Open to a **cash offer** (not just fishing for a number)
- Realistic on price (or coachable)

### 🚫 We Typically Pass On
- Sellers who just want retail value with no flexibility
- Properties with title issues that can't be resolved
- Deals where the numbers don't work at any reasonable price

---
