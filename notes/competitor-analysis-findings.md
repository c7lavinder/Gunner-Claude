# Competitor Call Analysis - Key Findings for Objection Handling Feature

## What They Do Well
The competitor analysis provides specific "potential replies" for different objection scenarios:

1. **Mirroring & Labeling Examples**: When seller says "there's a lot of memories there," suggest mirroring: "A lot of memories?" or labeling: "Sounds like those memories make it difficult to stay there."

2. **Price Objection Handling**: When seller says "I have no idea" about price, provide scripts like:
   - "If you had to guess, what do you think a house like yours in that area might be worth?"
   - "Have you looked at any comparable sales in your neighborhood?"
   - "Just so I can set expectations - our offers are typically 60-75% of retail value because we're paying cash and buying as-is. Would a number in that range work for your situation?"

3. **Pre-emptive Objection Handling**: Scripts to address objections before they arise:
   - "Mr. Broadway, I want to be upfront with you - because we're paying cash and buying as-is, our offer will be below what you might get if you listed with a realtor and waited for a retail buyer. The benefit is speed, convenience, and certainty. Is that trade-off something you'd consider?"

4. **Calibrated Questions**: Open-ended alternatives to yes/no questions:
   - "How long have you been thinking about selling?"
   - "What made you reach out to us specifically?"
   - "How has maintaining a nine-room house by yourself been?"
   - "What's driving the 60-day timeline?"
