# Lead Generator Cold Calling Script Analysis

## Script Structure (7 Sections)

### 1. Introduction
- Proper greeting with name and pause
- Identify property address
- Confirm if they're the owner

### 2. Initial Qualification
- Explain purpose (looking to buy property in area)
- Ask if interested in selling
- Handle "not interested" with future consideration question
- Offer $2,000 referral if still not interested

### 3. Setting Expectations
- Introduce working with private investor
- Explain cash offer, as-is, no closing cost
- Ask permission to ask questions
- Frame as "finding if we're a good fit"

### 4. Property and Seller Information
**Property Condition:**
- Confirm beds and baths
- Recent updates or big issues
- Occupancy status (owner-occupied, tenant, vacant)
- Repairs needed
- Tenant lease details if applicable

**Timeline:**
- How soon looking to sell (< 30 days, 2-3 months, 60+ days)

**Influencers:**
- Who has input on decision
- Anyone with veto power

**Motivation:**
- What prompted them to consider selling
- Dig deeper with follow-up questions:
  - "It seems like..."
  - "It feels like..."
  - "How long has this been going on?"
  - "Can you tell me more?"
  - "What have you tried?"
  - "How does that make you feel?"

### 5. Pricing Discussion
**Initial Price Inquiry:**
- Ask what they're looking to get
- If no price: ask for range
- If still no price: suggest 10-20k range to assist effectively

**Price Anchoring:**
- "Other investors buying at 40-50% of Zillow/Comparable Value"
- Clarify this is NOT the offer, just market context
- Handle objections ("way too low" / "that would work")
- Ask "what were you hoping I was going to at least say?"

**If Price Given:**
- "Is that the best you can do?"
- Confirm for finance department

### 6. Next Steps
**If Not a Fit:**
- Thank them, list with realtor or hold property
- Follow up in 30-60 days
- Save contact info

**If We Are a Fit (Transfer to Acquisition Manager):**
- Express fit and need to send to finance (30 mins)
- Schedule callback time
- Emphasize confident yes/no decision needed
- Mention other influencers should be on call
- Offer website for testimonials
- Confirm next steps and close

### 7. Objection Handling
- "I have a tenant in place" → ask lease terms
- "I don't need to sell" → question if should continue
- "I can list it on MLS" → ask why haven't listed yet
- "SMS lead asking price over market" → go into motivation ASAP
- "How'd you get my info?" → manager gives list, do you want to sell?
- "Why do you keep calling me?" → checking if looking to sell
- "I'm fixing it up myself" → sounds like going well, probably don't want to sell then?

## Grading Criteria for Lead Generator Calls

### A. Introduction & Rapport (15%)
- Proper greeting with name and pause
- Clear identification of property address
- Confirm owner status
- Professional and friendly tone

### B. Qualification & Interest (15%)
- Clearly explain purpose (buying property in area)
- Ask if interested in selling
- Handle objections appropriately
- Offer referral if not interested

### C. Setting Expectations (10%)
- Introduce private investor concept
- Explain cash, as-is, no closing cost offer
- Ask permission to ask questions
- Frame as "finding fit" not hard sell

### D. Information Gathering (25%)
- **Property details** (beds, baths, condition, occupancy, repairs)
- **Timeline** (urgency to sell)
- **Influencers** (decision makers, veto power)
- **Motivation** (why selling, dig deeper with follow-ups)

### E. Pricing Discussion (20%)
- Ask for seller's price expectation
- Handle "no price" with range questions
- Use price anchoring (40-50% Zillow) appropriately
- Clarify anchoring is NOT the offer
- Ask "what were you hoping I was going to at least say?"
- If price given: "Is that the best you can do?"

### F. Next Steps & Transfer (10%)
- Clear explanation of next steps (30 min callback with offer)
- Schedule specific callback time
- Emphasize need for confident yes/no decision
- Mention other influencers should be on call
- Offer website for testimonials
- Smooth handoff to Acquisition Manager

### G. Objection Handling (5%)
- Handle common objections professionally
- Use script responses appropriately
- Don't give up too easily
- Maintain professional tone even with difficult sellers

## Success Metrics
- **Lead Quality**: Did they gather enough info for AM to make offer?
- **Transfer Rate**: Did call result in scheduled callback with AM?
- **Script Adherence**: Did they follow the script flow?
- **Rapport Building**: Did they build trust and connection?
- **Motivation Discovery**: Did they uncover true motivation?
